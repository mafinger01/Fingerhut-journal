We arrived in the Jasper the hotel wasn’t the best because the wall were so thin and u could hear almost anything. Couple of us went for a drink and watch the hockey game in a bar. The next day we went hiking before we went we rented bear spray to protect us from any wildlife. The trail was close because of construction (building a reservoir for Caribou’s) so we drove a little bit till we saw a waterfall and decided to hiking around that area (fryatt trail). The trail end in on the other side of the construction site and but we didn’t know that.
